Hives!

The majority of what we do is look after our two hives, located right next to campus on Forsyth Avenue. This means opening up the two hives, making sure that there are no bad bugs or viruses, seeing if we can find the queen, and generally making sure that all is in order in the world of our colonies. We are currently looking to start a third colony in the spring of 2015. Added bonus: the hive will be located in Chancellor Wrighton's yard!

Honey!

We had our first honey extraction this fall, and boy oh boy was it good. We harvested over 15 pounds, enough to sell extra to students.
